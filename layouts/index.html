Stability LASSO

Based on the work from Liu et al., 2010, this method similar is to LASSO-StARS.

Graphical LASSO

This approach is called graphical LASSO and was developed by Friedman et al., 2008.

mLASSO-StARS

This method, developed by Miraldi et al., 2019 is called mLASSO-StARS and builds on LASSO-StARS.

GENIE3

As a comparison to the LASSO methods, we use GENIE3, a tree-based method from Huynh-Thu et al., 2010.

WGCNA

As another comparison to the LASSO methods, we consider WGCNA, a method from Langfelder & Horvath, 2008 that is based on correlation.

The Data

We have two primary data sources. The first was generated by Dr. Colin Kenny in Dr. Robert Cornell's lab at the University of Washington. The second source comes from Farrrel et al., 2018. Both are single-cell RNA-seq on zebrafish.
